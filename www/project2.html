RSVPer


One of the joys of sisterhood is being corralled into helping plan one’s sister’s wedding. That is the unenviable position I found myself in 2019. My sister was marrying her Prince Charming and I was in charge of heralding it to the world via invites. Thankfully I had a lot of help sending out the invites, however consolidating the RSVPs was another matter. Especially since there were 600 guests attending 3 different events.

That is when I created RSVPer.

RSVPer is an SMS based chatbot that uses natural language processing (NLP) to read and respond to RSVP responses from invitees without any human intervention.

Currently, RSVPer is in closed beta-testing. Please contact me if you would like to participate.
