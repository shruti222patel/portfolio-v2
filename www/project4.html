Kennedy Children's Center


I am proud to announce that I have joined the Kennedy Children’s Center (KCC) as a Board Member! KCC provides special education services to nearly 400 preschoolers in East Harlem in the Bronx, while training members of our diverse community to enter the field of early childhood special education. Working closely with families, we provide evidence-based, culturally-appropriate services in traditionally underserved communities.

As a member of KCC’s Board of Directors, I use my technical skills and out-of-the-box strategizing to advise KCC on program initiatives that may fall outside normal operations. Currently, I am on the Strategic Planning and Finance committees, helping facilitate a partnership between KCC and an edu tech company.
